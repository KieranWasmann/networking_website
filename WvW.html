Wired Vs Wireless Connections

There are many pros and cons with both wired and wireless connections.

Wired

Wired connections are great in the fact that their fast and reliable. They are less likely to fail, but require more effort to connect and produce a connection that requires the device to stay stationary.

Wireless

Wireless connections are not as fast as a wired connection, however they remain mobile and are easier to connect to. They are not as secure and can be joined by devices that are not desired to be part of the network.
